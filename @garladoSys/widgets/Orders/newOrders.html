About Client Orders

From here you get to view the following
  1. View Orders Placed
    You are able to view the orders placed by the client through the Client Orders Tab. You will also see the total amount of the Order Placed
  2. View Client Info
    You will be able to see the client information through the Client Details Tab. From Here you will be able to contact the user and make delivery arrangements
  3. View Client Location
    You will be able to see a map location of where your client is through the Client Location's Tab. This should make it easy to deliver their products. If the Client's location provided is not clear, the area will be indicated by a line path.
  4. Actions
    Here you will be able to act on the client order through the Action's Tab. From here, you will see the client address to deliver too,payment status and a group of options. Click on the appropriate action.
