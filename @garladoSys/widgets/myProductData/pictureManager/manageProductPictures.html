About Manage Product Pictures

Here you get to specifically manage a product's images. You can either delete an image or set an image to bee seen on the web. To do so:
  1. Click on an image to select it. If it appears under the product title, it is selected.
  2. Click on an action. Delete to delete the image, Set for web to set it for your website.
  3. Thats all!
