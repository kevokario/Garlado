About Add Product

Here you get to add your products. to do so, follow the below steps.
  1. Select General group for your product
  2. Select Category group for your product
  3. Select the Specific group for your product
  4. Enter product name
  5. Enter sell price for your product
  6. Enter the market price for your product
  7. Select a suitable picture for your product
  8. Select a brand that your product belongs to
  9. Set a status for your product
  10. Click the add Button!
